ESLint v5.10.0 released

We just pushed ESLint v5.10.0, which is a minor release upgrade of ESLint. This release adds some new features and fixes several bugs found in the previous release.

Highlights

  • The padding-line-between-statements rule has new statement types: multiline-const, multiline-let, multiline-var, singleline-const, singleline-let, and singleline-var.
  • The require-jsdoc and valid-jsdoc rules have been deprecated, following our previously announced decision to end support for JSDoc in ESLint.
